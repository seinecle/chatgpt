ChatGPT: in higher education and beyond

[work in progress, come back often for new material!]

This content is designed by Clement Levallois, Associate Professor at emlyon business school.

The targeted audience are actors in higher education such as students, program directors, deans, and professors. I hope this can also be useful beyong higher education, and serve parents, families, employees and anyone interested - and worried? - about the impact of ChatGPT on their lives.

The materials presented here are my (Clement Levallois') opinions. They do not represent a policy by the school.
